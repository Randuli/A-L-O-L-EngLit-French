Exam Preparation Tips

Boost your performance in O/L English with these practical tips!

Despite being an interesting subject, O/L English Literature is quite arduous. Thus, it is important to follow an effective plan

  • Revise repeatedly: There are 20 poems, 4 short stories, 2 dramas and 1 novel for you to study. So, don't wait till the last moment
  • Practice essays: You must try to write 3 pages in 30 mins which means you HAVE to practice a lot
  • Expand vocabulary: Literature is after all , the magical creation of words!
  • Analyze everything: Identify themes and techniques in each area (poetry, prose, etc.).
  • Believe in yourself: Confidence is key to success!
