The Syllabus

The below are the main lessons that you should know as an O/L French Student

Having a solid understanding on the following will help you get an A PASS for French

  • Main Tenses: Le Présent, Le Passé Composé, L'Imparfait, Le Présent Progressif, Le Conditionnel Présent
  • Other "tenses": Le Futur Proche, Le Passé Récent, La Forme Interrogative, L'Impératif
  • Articles: Definite articles, Indefinite articles, Partitive articles
  • Grammar lessons:Nouns, Adjectives, Adverbs, Prepositions, Pronominal and Reflexive verbs, Comparative and Superlative, Les Pronoms Relatifs, les Pronoms Possessifs, le pronom EN & Y, COD: Complément d'Objet Direct, COI: Complément d'Objet Indirect
  • Other lessons: Countries, Numbers, Colours, Days of the Week, Months of the Year, Telling Time, Clothes, Occupations, Instruments -> Learn these in French!
